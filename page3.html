About us


    Since 1970, ARK MARINE SERVICE INC. has been dealing with spare parts dealers, middleman and trading companies operating in Japan. We specialize in original equipment for main and auxiliary engine parts, we can also supply certificates if required like NK report, maker's genuine certificate and others.

   To serve you better and to meet your high expections, we have employed in our staff specialists in the marine business industry who are fully trained in the business. We also provide domestic delivery, Logistic Support, courier services delivery, Ship on Board Delivery, Air Freight and Sea Freight worldwide via international airports and seaports.
    
   On top of that, the rates we offer are equitable yet not compromising high standards. Please feel free to communicate with us, our English speaking staff are always available to receive and answer your enquiries.
